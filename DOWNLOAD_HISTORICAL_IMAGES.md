## How to Download

1. Open each URL above in your browser
2. Right-click on the image → "Save Image As..."
3. Navigate to `/Users/nathanielrichmond/jewish-cultural-map/public/historical/`
4. Save with the exact filename shown (1.jpg, 2.jpg, etc.)
5. Replace the existing placeholder files

All images are public domain from Wikimedia Commons.
